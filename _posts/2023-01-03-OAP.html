For it, I made print and digital materials to market the events" description: " The tagline our team collaborated on for this event was 'see me, hear me, feel me', to bring awareness to erasure of community voices and the importance of understanding each individual's story in the community. The Out and Proud Series presents the largest opportunity for community visibility on the UC San Diego campus. It is a time for reflection on the achievements of LGBTQIA+ identified students, staff, faculty, and our allies, and also a time for public awareness and discussion of current LGBTQIA+ issues on the state and national levels." img: /assets/images/OAP-Calendar.png sidebar-img: /assets/images/OAP-pin.png designation:
